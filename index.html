Useful Advice

Quick Learning Tips

🌅

Practice in the morning

🗣️

Talk to yourself

Describe your surroundings in English. Sounds weird, works brilliantly.

📓

Keep a vocab notebook

Write 5 new words each day with a sentence. Review on weekends.

🎬

Watch with subtitles
